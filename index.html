  • About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Jayce, a Project Coordinator with a strong passion for the environmental and animal welfare data. I'm thrilled that you've taken the time to explore my work.

Welcome to my portfolio, a curated collection of my data analysis projects that bring my skills to life in diverse real-world contexts. From conducting environmental impact assessments to driving initiatives for animal welfare, I am thrilled to present the results of my data-driven inquiries. With over two years of dedicated experience in data entry and visualizations, I am resolutely committed to harnessing the transformative potential of data analysis to further environmental sustainability and enhance animal welfare practices. My proficiency spans project coordination, data visualization, data cleaning, and report generation, enabling me to create impactful solutions and insights through data-driven methodologies.

Thank you once again for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my data-driven journey toward a better world!
